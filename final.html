"A year-round Christmas Morning"

Food for thought

Ask any mom and they'll tell you toddlers have a sixth sense when they arrive at the doctors office.

These children know the needle police is around the corner. Children hospitals around the country treat about ONE MILLION outpatients yearly. Unfortunately, some treatments require our small superheroes to stay longterm. Statistically, urban hospitals have 500-600 pediatric patients a night.


Now think of your happiest memory as a child. Did it happen to involve Christmas morning, or your birthday? PRESENTS, immediately will induce a smile from anyone.


The point of this organization is to replace at least one bad memory in a child with one of opening a gift. It may seem trivial but for a young child battling one of the following monsters: bone cancer, genetic disorders, hepatoblatoma, leukemia, lymphoma, neuroblastoma, skin cancer, soft tissue diseases, thyroid disease, and Wilms disease, a small gift may give them a boost to keep going.

Our promise

While we would love to accept any gifts you may have, the children we donate to have very weak immune systems. Our liaisons from each hospital individually approves each donation we make to make sure they are not a threat to the health of the children. We are not able to accept gifts not pre-approved by Cresent Giving or the hospital staff.

LOVE

All we want is for these little superheroes to feel completely LOVED.

GREEN
